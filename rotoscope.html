1-Minute Video Rotoscope

Weekly production breakdown from Week 1 to Week 12 with step-by-step mini tutorials, YouTube references, and 8 panel guides with visual examples.

Weekly Progress (Week 1-12)

Week 1: Concept and Storyboard

Watch Week 1 Guide
  1. Choose a 1-minute story idea.
  2. Create thumbnail storyboard frames.
  3. Define key actions and camera angles.

Week 2: Video Shoot Planning

Watch Week 2 Guide
  1. Prepare a shot list for every scene.
  2. Set lighting and background consistency.
  3. Record reference footage in stable framing.

Week 3: Frame Extraction

Watch Week 3 Guide
  1. Import video into editing software.
  2. Export frames at selected intervals.
  3. Organize files by scene and frame number.

Week 4: Base Drawing Pass

Watch Week 4 Guide
  1. Draw simple outlines over extracted frames.
  2. Focus on silhouette and proportion.
  3. Check smoothness between adjacent frames.

Week 5: Clean Line Work

Watch Week 5 Guide
  1. Refine rough strokes into clean lines.
  2. Use consistent brush settings.
  3. Fix jitter and shape inconsistencies.

Week 6: Color Blocking

Watch Week 6 Guide
  1. Create palette for characters and props.
  2. Fill major shapes with flat colors.
  3. Maintain frame-to-frame color consistency.

Week 7: Shading and Highlights

Watch Week 7 Guide
  1. Define one clear light direction.
  2. Add shadows to create depth.
  3. Apply highlights on key surfaces.

Week 8: Background Integration

Watch Week 8 Guide
  1. Paint or compose background elements.
  2. Match perspective and horizon lines.
  3. Blend character edges with environment.

Week 9: In-Between Frames

Watch Week 9 Guide
  1. Add in-betweens for smoother movement.
  2. Watch timing on fast actions.
  3. Adjust spacing for natural motion arcs.

Week 10: Compositing and Effects

Watch Week 10 Guide
  1. Stack rotoscope layers and masks.
  2. Add subtle glow or grain effects.
  3. Balance contrast and color grade.

Week 11: Audio and Final Edit

Watch Week 11 Guide
  1. Sync soundtrack and sound effects.
  2. Trim sequences to exactly one minute.
  3. Export a high-quality preview.

Week 12: Review and Publish

Watch Week 12 Guide
  1. Collect critique and check pacing.
  2. Fix final technical and artistic issues.
  3. Render and publish your final cut.
